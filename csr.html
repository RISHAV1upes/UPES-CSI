UPES-CSI,CSR

UPES CSI-CSR believes in working for a cause because we don't need a reason to help people. The sole purpose of CSR is to lay emphasis on social work and issues and to spread awareness among the students.The chapter involves in CSR activities and works towards making Dehradun “Computer Literate” by organizing study camps for underprivileged students. UPES CSI organized an event “Human The Virtue” which provided a platform to the student of Clodine International School to showcase their skills. 'Love, happiness, kindness and generosity all together makes cherishing memorable moments'. Orphanage drive on Children's Day, Old age home visit on Independence day are some of the wonderful initiatives taken by UPES CSI-CSR.
